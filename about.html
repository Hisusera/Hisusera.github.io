Strawberry

A perennial herbaceous plant. Its fruits are usually eaten-tubers of rounded shape, after which the plant was named. The berries have a pronounced red color, do not exceed 5 g in weight. You can recognize the fruits by their specific smell: a mixture of honey, musk and wine.
